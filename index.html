Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and services

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, and payments via WhatsApp. For international orders, we also accept PayPal.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery within Lagos typically takes 1-2 business days. For other states, delivery takes 3-5 business days depending on your location.

Are your products organic?

All our products are made from natural ingredients. While not all are officially certified organic, they are grown without artificial pesticides or chemicals.

What is the shelf life of your products?

Our oils typically last 12-18 months when stored properly in a cool, dark place. Date products have a shelf life of about 6 months.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship internationally. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please contact us for specific details.
